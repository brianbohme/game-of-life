Game of Life



Instructions: Press 'random' to generate a random board, else click on the board to toggle live and dead cells. 'Step' moves the game once, while 'play' autoplays the game until you press 'play' again to pause the game. Press clear to produce an empty board.

Blue cells are alive. White cells are dead. A live cell with less than 2 cells around it will die of underpopulation. A live cell with more than 3 cells around it will die of overcrowding. A dead cell will stay dead unless surrounded by 3 living neighbors (out of 8), in which it'll be reborn.
